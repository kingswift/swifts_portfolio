Reviews

Drake

Views is an okay album. I have purchased this album based off of the hype. After listening to the album, it was definitely not worth the purchase. There are a few good songs on the album, most notably “Weston Road Flows” , “Redemption” and “With You”. I think Drake’s attempt at a new style ruined it for me. I think he should have kept the same formula he used for the “Nothing Was The Same” album.

Rihanna

Rihanna's album Anti is a very solid album. There are a few amazing tracks : “Yeah I Said It” , “Needed Me”, “Sex With Me”, “Higher”, “Love On The Brain”. In all I fully recommend purchasing this album. This is one of Rihanna’s greatest work to date. The beats are amazing and she has developed her own distinct sound on this album.

Twenty88

Twenty88 is a great duo album. Based off of some of the past work that Big Sean and Jhene Aiko have done together I knew there would be a chance for success. Boy was I right!!! This album has a very futuristic yet throwback rap/rnb feel to it. My favorite songs are: “Memories Faded”, “Déjà vu”, “On The Way”, “Talk Show” and “2 Minute Warning”. This is an album I recommend to all the throwback 80’s hip hip and rnb heads.
