About

Hello World, Soundtrack is a new website which allows music enthusiast to view my honest critique of new music. Weekly, I will post photo stills of newly released albums and critique them. Based on my reviews, you will be able to determine if the album is worth listening to before purchasing or downloading. Also, don’t be afraid to send request for albums or songs you would like me to review. Thanks for your support and enjoy the website.
